The problem

Over time, engines choke themselves.

Even with the best servicing, carbon builds up where it does the most damage. Combustion chambers, valves, fuel injectors, EGR valves and exhaust systems all clog over the miles. Power drops, fuel economy slips and emissions climb.

It isn't only wear and tear. The engine is slowly choking itself.

The fix

A clean, not a strip-down.

Our hydrogen-based process clears that carbon out without removing a single component. It takes as little as an hour per vehicle, so downtime stays to a minimum.

  • Restores power and performance
  • Restores mpg by up to 15%
  • Removes carbon deposits
  • Reduces exhaust emissions
  • Prolongs component life
  • Lowers engine running temperatures
  • Works as early intervention before bigger build-ups
  • Helps pinpoint faults behind carbon build-up

What this isn't

We'll tell you straight.

No manufacturer will tell you their engine carbons up, but it's a fact of how combustion engines work over time. Where there's a build-up or blockage, there's often an underlying fault behind it.

We identify. We don't repair. Our process is excellent at helping identify an underlying fault behind carbon build-up. It won't fix that fault. We're not a repair shop, and we don't claim to be. What we do is clear the carbon, restore performance, and tell you straight if something else needs a look.
